General Overview

Welcome to the Apostle Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering, a distinctive 10-week ministry dedicated to guiding students with aspirations in technology. We understand that transitioning from academic life to a professional career in tech can be daunting, especially when many essential skills for the tech industry aren't covered in traditional academia. Our ministry is designed to alleviate the anxieties associated with securing internships, jobs, or research opportunities by fostering a supportive community where you can navigate these challenges alongside your peers. Moreover, we are committed to exploring how we can honor and glorify God through our academic pursuits and professional endeavors in the tech field.

Projects

Another question often asked by college students is how to make their applications stand out without any real job experience. One of the best ways to achieve this is through side projects that showcase your skills and passions. This ministry will offer three opportunities to work on projects that may enhance your resume. These projects will cover fundamental aspects of software engineering, including web development, data analysis, and machine learning.

  • Web App Development
  • Data Analysis
  • Machine Learning
